# Third-Person Survival Arena

A Unity survival game where you navigate a dangerous terrain filled with enemies, mines, and obstacles to reach the end goal.

## Controls

### Movement
- **Z** - Move Forward
- **S** - Move Backward
- **Q** - Move Left
- **D** - Move Right
- **Space** - Jump
- **Mouse** - Look around and aim

### Combat
- **Left Mouse Button** - Throw Grenade

### Other
- **Escape** - Pause Menu

### Key Items
- **Key** - Required to activate the elevator and complete the level. Located in the central area of the map.

### Pickups
- **Health Packs** - Restore 25 HP. Scattered throughout the terrain.

### Hazards
- **Mines** - Explode on contact with player or enemies. Marked by trigger zones on the ground. Deal 10 damage in a 2-unit radius.
- **Enemy Grenadiers** - Enemies that follow and throw grenades at the player. Deal damage and can be eliminated with grenades or environmental hazards.

## Objectives

1. Navigate the terrain and avoid mines
2. Collect the Key
3. Defeat or avoid enemy grenadiers
4. Reach the End objective (big easter egg) to win
